FOR BOOKINGS

We are open from Tuesday to Sunday from 7 pm for dinner,
and from 12:30 - 2:30 pm on Sundays for lunch.

For reservations,
call between 12:00 pm to 8:00 pm on +91 98190 69222,

or email bookings@masquerestaurant.com.

Our 10-course tasting menu is priced at Rs. 5200*.

Please note we require an advance deposit to confirm all tables.

Masque gift cards are also available. Please email us to make a purchase.

 

We offer seasonal tasting menus and encourage you to share any allergies or dietary restrictions so we can tailor the menu to your preference.


*Price excludes service charge and government taxes as applicable.
